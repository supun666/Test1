paragraph is a group of sentences that develop a single main idea.
A well-structured paragraph typically includes a topic sentence, supporting sentences, and a concluding
sentence. For example, a paragraph about the benefits of exercise might begin with a topic sentence
stating that exercise improves physical and mental health, then provide supporting sentences detailing
specific benefits like increased cardiovascular health and reduced stress, and conclude with a
sentence summarizing the overall positive impact of exercise.
